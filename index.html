About Being a Man With no Land

For a long time I was feeling like I don't belong to a place, and it made me feel really guilty. However, not really belonging to anywhere has given me freedom to live and to love without limits, no matter where I am.

My parents come from different states in Colombia, and I was born in the capital city, where neither of them belongs to. I was the only child in my family that was born in that city and just a year after I was born we moved away from it. My father got a job in his hometown and we spent the next 17 years living there. However, I never could feel like I fitted in my father's city. It was a really nice town though , it is placed by the Caribbean Sea and the Landscapes that surround it are really interesting.
